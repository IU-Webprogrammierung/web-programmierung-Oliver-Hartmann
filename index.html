Studio

Modular synthesizers are electronic musical instruments composed of separate modules that can be connected in various ways to create custom soundscapes. I call this freedom in the creative process. The computer I use mainly as multitrack recorder.

About Me

Since the late 90s I produce electronic music under my artist name Insect O. My music is inspired by ambient and dub techno. Most of my music has been released on my own label Etui Records. I call it music for travelling. Since 2022 I'm studying media technology at IU - Internationale Hochschule.
