Live Performances

I play electronic music live. For my performances I use my modular synthesizers, analog drum maschines and effect pedals. Together with visual artist highpitchedblack I work on AV live shows for DAVE Festival.

Workshops

In my lectures I talk about music production and live performing. But also the influence of artificial inteligence on music is a hot topic people want to discuss.

About Me

Since the late 90s I produce electronic music under my artist name Insect O. My music is inspired by ambient and dub techno. Most of my music has been released on my own label Etui Records. I call it music for travelling. Since 2022 I'm studying media technology at IU - Internationale Hochschule.
