About the release

Follow Insect O. on a journey to ambient music and electronica. Raw tones from analog oscillators are blending with digital sound sources and field recordings to peel something beautiful.

The music is inspired by the wild landscape of Patagonia. For “Coiba” he recorded evolving chords of the Plaits module going through Make Noise qpas and strings of the Prophet 6 synthesizer. On top he layered field recordings of the ocean he took at the untouched island of Coiba.

It’s time to dream and relax.
